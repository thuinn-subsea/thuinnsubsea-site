About Thuinn Subsea

Thuinn Subsea Limited supports subsea and offshore projects with a focus on ROV operations, technical assurance, equipment readiness and clear project delivery.

Alongside operational support, Thuinn Subsea is developing practical software tools for offshore professionals, helping users manage records, evidence, certificates and project information more effectively.

Digital development

Apps built for offshore users

Thuinn Subsea is developing digital tools designed around real offshore workflows, not generic office processes.

The focus is on simple, practical apps that help offshore professionals organise records, capture evidence, manage certificates and produce useful outputs while working in demanding offshore environments.

Current development focus

Offshore Logbook is a local-first iOS app for offshore, subsea and ROV professionals to record work history, manage certificates, attach evidence and export professional logbook records.

App support and privacy information are provided separately for users and App Store review.

Built from field experience

Digital tools shaped around the kind of information offshore users actually need to capture and reuse.

Local-first approach

Preference for privacy-aware, user-controlled tools that work well without relying on constant connectivity.

Professional outputs

Focus on structured records, evidence, exports and documentation that support professional workflows.
