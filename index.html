Offshore Logbook

Offshore Logbook helps offshore, subsea and ROV professionals keep structured career records on device. Record trips, roles, vessels, ROV systems, certificates, verifier signatures, evidence files and export-ready career summaries.

Built around a private, local-first approach: your records stay on your device unless you choose to export, back up or share them.

Logbook Records

Track offshore work history, roles, vessels, projects, dates and technical experience.

Evidence Attachments

Attach supporting images, PDFs, signatures and certificate evidence to your records.

Professional Exports

Create CV-style summaries, work matrices and PDF logbook exports for professional use.

Local-First Privacy

No app-managed accounts, no backend storage and no automatic upload of personal records.

Designed for Offshore Professionals

Suitable for ROV personnel, subsea teams, survey, inspection, deck crew, client representatives, offshore managers and other offshore professionals who need organised career records.
