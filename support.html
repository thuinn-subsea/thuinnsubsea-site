What Offshore Logbook Supports

  • Offshore work history tracking
  • ROV and subsea logbook management
  • Certificate, training and competence records
  • Evidence image and PDF attachments
  • Verifier and offshore manager signatures
  • CV, work matrix and PDF exports
  • Local backup and restore

Troubleshooting

If exports, backups or evidence files are not appearing correctly, please restart the app and try again.

If issues continue, contact support with:

  • Device model
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps taken before the issue occurred

Privacy Reminder

Offshore Logbook is designed as a local-first app. Records are stored on your device unless you choose to export, back up or share them.
