Privacy Policy

Offshore Logbook stores logbook entries, profile details, certificates, evidence files, signatures and settings locally on the user's device.

The app does not operate a backend service, create user accounts or automatically upload personal data.

Users may choose to export PDFs, CVs, backups or feedback emails using Apple sharing tools. Any sharing action is initiated by the user.

Subscription validation is handled through Apple services.
