Privacy Policy

Offshore Logbook by Thuinn Subsea

Local-First Data Storage

Offshore Logbook stores logbook entries, profile details, certificates, evidence files, signatures and settings locally on the user's device.

The app does not operate a backend service, create app-managed user accounts or automatically upload personal logbook data.

User-Controlled Sharing

Users may choose to export PDFs, CVs, backups or feedback emails using Apple sharing tools. Any sharing action is initiated by the user.

Users are responsible for reviewing exported records before sharing them professionally.

Subscriptions

Subscription validation and purchase management are handled through Apple services. Offshore Logbook does not directly collect payment card details.
